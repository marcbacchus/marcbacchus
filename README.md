## What I do

I design and build enterprise data platforms for regulated industries — pharma, life sciences, managed care. Two decades at Pfizer, embedded through IntegriChain, Cumberland, and Mindlance: cloud migrations, analytics platforms supporting $1.5B+ in contract decisions, automated pipelines replacing 25+ hours of manual work weekly.

The work I'm best known for: an Oracle → Snowflake migration across 200+ tables that cut query latency by 60%, and an enterprise analytics platform that gave finance and managed care leadership real-time visibility where they had weekly Excel cycles before.

I also teach graduate data courses at NYU's School of Professional Studies — which means I spend equal time building production systems and explaining the tradeoffs behind them clearly.
